Welcome to Trabs

Trabs.ng is Nigeria’s foremost online bus ticket booking and management platform with its concentration on people and convenient service delivery. The Trabs.ng ticket booking platform was borne with the aim to offer a very convenient system of bus ticket booking and management services for travellers and bus transport operators.

Established in 2020, Trabs.ng has set out itself to be a leading online ticket booking and management platform with specialization in providing travellers convenience in booking their tickets online, minimal waiting time, cashless bookings and secure payment systems while offering the bus transport operators a general reduction in cost of operations giving rise to paperless ticket bookings, ease in preparation and generation of manifest and easy payment procedures.

With the advance in technology, Trabs.ng uses cutting edge technology to serve its customers (travellers and bus transport operators) with ease and the satisfaction of an optimum engagement.

“Think TRABS think of a smart convenient way of booking your travels”.

Privacy policy

Visiting this web site does not oblige the user to provide any personal information. However, in order to provide our products and services, we need to process certain personal data such as your name, email address, telephone number, travel preferences etc.

Trabs.ng uses these data for commercial purposes, operations and statistics. The voluntary or obligatory nature of the data being gathered will be indicated at the time the process of data gathering is taking place. The refusal to provide obligatory details will imply non-performance or denial of access to the service for which they were required.

The transmission of your credit card information, personal data and any other information is conducted under a secure environment, using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protocol. The secure server establishes a connection so that the information is transmitted in an encrypted format.
